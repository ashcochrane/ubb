Set your default margin
When a customer uses your product, we calculate the API cost, add your margin, and debit the total from their Stripe balance. Set a default margin now — you can refine this per product or per pricing card later once you've created them.
Default margin
This margin will apply to all API costs across all products and pricing cards you create. You can override it at the product or card level anytime after setup.
50%
A 50% margin means a $2.00 API cost is charged to your customer as $3.00. Your profit on that request is $1.00.
